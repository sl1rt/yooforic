Yes, it’s legal in all 50 states! The 2018 U.S. Farm Bill, which was passed into law by President Trump on December 20, 2018, allowed for the legalization of hemp cultivation and sale of hemp-based products.

Can I get high from full spectrum hemp oil?
Our full spectrum hemp oil comes from carefully-cultivated hemp plants-not marijuana—which is why it is legal for us to import and use this natural full-spectrum hemp oil. Our supply partners spent years developing special cultivars of the hemp that produce naturally occurring phytocompounds and only trace amounts of THC, the psychoactive compound that gives marijuana users the "high" feeling. In comparison to hemp, most recreational cannabis plants have between 5% to over 20% THC. The hemp oil used in Yooforic chewing gum contains less than 0.1% THC, which is essentially none!

Why is Hemp Oil Gum better than Gummies, Capsules, Vapes, and Tinctures?
Chewing Gum is a superior delivery method for maximum absorption. When you chew a piece of Yooforic Gum full-spectrum hemp oil loaded with an entourage of powerful phytocompounds is released. This allows for quick absorption and maintained bioavailability. Gummies and capsules are swallowed and move through the digestive system. The digestive system naturally breaks down the hemp oil quickly, which greatly reduces the amount that’s actually absorbed.
